# Evaluation

## Requirements for Maximum Grade 9

To obtain a maximum grade of **9**:

- Develop a project.
- Form a team of **7 members**.
- Choose a subject from the available psychology topics.
- Complete the assigned tasks throughout the semester.
- Ensure that each team member presents at least one assignment.

## Requirements for Grade 10

To obtain a grade of **10**:

- Prepare an extra report on a subject provided by the instructor.
- Present the report during the semester at a chosen presentation date.
